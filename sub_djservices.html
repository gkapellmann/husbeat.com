The Infohub

The infohub project is designed to have easy access to information about the weather, bus schedule, and time. Perfect for when you are about to get ready and leave the house.

The main focus of the project was to learn new skills within 3D-modeling / CAD design, product design, and a bit of software development. The CAD design software used for the project was Onshape, and Magic Mirror as the monitor interface. The modules used for the infohub are the built in clock-module, MMM-YrNow, and MMM-Entur-tavle.

The Table-in-a-Drawer

The Table-in-a-Drawer gives the IKEA Alex drawer unit an extra functionality, a mini-table hidden inside the top drawer.

The extra table is great for having a morning tea, lunch, and a notebook by my side while working. Freeing up space around the keyboard and the mouse.
